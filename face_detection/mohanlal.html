On Mohanlal’s 61st birthday, take a look at his 4 luxury cars & why they’re perfect for The Complete Actor

All white, all day, errday.

By Yash Bharati

21 May 2021

Superstar Mohanlal turns 61 today – and looks as young as ever. In Drishyam 2, one of his newest releases, Mohanlal played Georgekutty and picked up exactly where had left off the first instalment of the riveting thriller. But today, we won't talk about his movies. Here, we're going to acquaint you with a few of his luxurious rides. 

On Mohanlal’s 61st birthday, here are his 4 luxury cars & why they’re perfect for the Universal Superstar

1. Toyota Vellfire
Advertisement

Tall, robust and rare the Toyota Vellfire isn’t one that you would see often on the road. Making its way to Mohanlal’s garage for Rs 79 lakh, per HT Auto, the car beams brightly in a sleek white finish. One of the most luxurious and spacious cars, Vellfire is designed keeping in mind the actor's privacy and long travels. With ample space in the back seat, a divider sheet between the driver and the back, and ultra-comfortable seating, the ride is best suited for Mohanlal.

2. Toyota Land Cruiser

The Land Cruiser is another beastly vehicle from Toyota, which seems to be a brand that Mohanlal is loyal to. He owns a Toyota Innova. A favourite among politicians and actors, the SUV is a preferred choice of many for long drives, is classy and Mohanlal loves it in a white finish, just like his other rides.

3. Mercedes Benz GL350

Another towering SUV, this time from Mercedes, the Mercedes Benz GL 350 is a hot favourite among Mohanlal’s rides, per Cartoq. The 61-year-old actor has been spotted arriving at shoots and events in this robust SUV.

4. Lamborghini Urus

Lamborghini Urus, an SUV from the Italian carmaker which Kartik Aaryan also added to his collection recently, finds a place in Mohanlal’s garage too in white finish.
